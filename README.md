# Olist Store EDA

Exploratory Data Analysis of Olist Store orders. 
The analysis explores customer behavior, product performance, order dynamics, revenue, and reviews.

## Data
The dataset contains real orders from Olist Store, a Brazilian e-commerce company, from 2016 to 2018.
It includes:
- Customer information (`customers.csv`)
- Order details (`orders.csv`, `order_items.csv`, `order_payments.csv`)
- Product information (`products.csv`, `product_category_name_translation.csv`)
- Order reviews (`order_reviews.csv`)

## Analysis
The project is organized into the following sections:
1. **Distribution of order statuses** – proportions of delivered, canceled, and unavailable orders.  
2. **Number of customer orders** – how many orders each customer places.  
3. **Distribution of payment types** – e.g., credit card, boleto.  
4. **Distribution of payment installments** – how orders are split into installments.  
5. **Revenue analysis** – overall revenue patterns.  
6. **Revenue dependence on payment installments** – correlation between order value and number of installments.  
7. **Order and revenue statistics by purchase month** – monthly trends.  
8. **Delivery analysis** – delivery times and delays.  
9. **Items price** – distribution of product prices.  
10. **Distribution of items counts in an order** – how many items per order.  
11. **Items popularity** – which items are most frequently sold.  
12. **Product categories analysis** – products and revenue by category.  
13. **Aggregated metrics by customer city** – orders and revenue by customer location.  
14. **Customers and sellers states distribution** – geographic distribution of participants.  
15. **Review score changes** – dynamics of customer review scores.  
16. **Score dependence on comment** – how review content affects scores.  
17. **Review answer time dependence on score** – response times from sellers.  
18. **Revenue dependence on score** – connection between review score and order value.
